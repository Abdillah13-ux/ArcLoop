# ArcLoop Grant Fit

ArcLoop is designed for practical USDC utility on Arc.

## Why ArcLoop Fits

- Uses USDC as the contribution and payout asset.
- Uses Arc Testnet as the settlement layer.
- Uses a live smart contract for pool coordination and payout logic.
- Uses Circle User-Controlled Wallets for consumer-friendly onboarding.
- Provides verifiable contribution and payout proofs.

## Category Positioning

ArcLoop is positioned as a consumer/community savings coordination app, not as:
- an AI-agent payment app
- a remittance app
- a generic wallet
- a gambling or lottery product
- a yield or investment product

## Core Grant Narrative

ArcLoop brings rotating savings circles on-chain with transparent USDC settlement, fixed contribution rounds, predefined payout order, and verifiable payout proofs on Arc.
